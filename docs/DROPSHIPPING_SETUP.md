## Step 2: Install Your Dropshipping App

### For DSers (Recommended for AliExpress):

1. **Go to Shopify Admin**
   - Navigate to: `https://by2050-test.myshopify.com/admin`
   - Log in with your credentials

2. **Install DSers**
   - Go to **Apps** ‚Üí **Visit Shopify App Store**
   - Search for "DSers"
   - Click **Add app** ‚Üí **Install app**
   - Authorize the app to access your store

3. **Create DSers Account**
   - Sign up at [dsers.com](https://www.dsers.com)
   - Choose a plan (Free plan works to start)
   - Connect your Shopify store

4. **Connect AliExpress**
   - In DSers dashboard, click **Connect AliExpress**
   - Log in with your AliExpress account (create one if needed)
   - Authorize DSers to access AliExpress

### For Spocket (US/EU Suppliers):

1. **Install Spocket**
   - Go to **Apps** ‚Üí **Visit Shopify App Store**
   - Search for "Spocket"
   - Click **Add app** ‚Üí **Install app**

2. **Create Spocket Account**
   - Sign up at [spocket.co](https://www.spocket.co)
   - Choose a plan
   - Connect your Shopify store

3. **Browse Suppliers**
   - Browse US/EU suppliers in Spocket
   - Filter by category, shipping time, location

## Step 3: Import Products

### Using DSers (AliExpress):

1. **Find Products on AliExpress**
   - Browse AliExpress for products you want to sell
   - Look for products with:
     - ‚úÖ Good reviews (4+ stars)
     - ‚úÖ High order volume
     - ‚úÖ Fast shipping options
     - ‚úÖ Reliable suppliers

2. **Import to DSers**
   - Install DSers browser extension (Chrome/Firefox)
   - On AliExpress product page, click **Add to DSers**
   - Or copy product URL and paste in DSers dashboard

3. **Edit Product Details**
   - **Title**: Make it SEO-friendly and brand-appropriate
   - **Description**: Rewrite to match your brand voice
   - **Price**: Set your markup (recommended: 2-3x cost)
   - **Images**: Use supplier images or add your own
   - **Variants**: Configure sizes, colors, etc.

4. **Push to Shopify**
   - Click **Push to Shopify** in DSers
   - Product appears in your Shopify store
   - Edit further in Shopify if needed

### Using Spocket (US/EU):

1. **Browse Spocket Catalog**
   - Log into Spocket dashboard
   - Browse products by category
   - Filter by location, shipping time, price

2. **Import Products**
   - Click **Import** on products you want
   - Edit product details (title, description, price)
   - Click **Push to Shopify**

3. **Review in Shopify**
   - Go to **Products** in Shopify admin
   - Review imported products
   - Add to collections

## Step 4: Set Up Collections

1. **Create Collections**
   - Go to **Products** ‚Üí **Collections**
   - Click **Create collection**
   - Create collections like:
     - "Shirts"
     - "Trousers"
     - "Outerwear"
     - "New Arrivals"
     - "Best Sellers"

2. **Add Products to Collections**
   - Select products
   - Click **More actions** ‚Üí **Add to collection**
   - Choose collections

3. **Link Collections to Homepage**
   - Go to **Online Store** ‚Üí **Themes** ‚Üí **Customize**
   - Find **Collection Tiles** section
   - Select your collections
   - Save

---

## Step 5: Configure Order Fulfillment

### DSers Auto-Fulfillment:

1. **Enable Auto-Order**
   - In DSers dashboard, go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Auto Order**
   - Enable **Auto Order** toggle
   - Set payment method (credit card/PayPal)

2. **Configure Order Rules**
   - Set minimum order value (if any)
   - Choose shipping method preference
   - Set inventory sync rules

3. **Test Order**
   - Place a test order in your store
   - Verify DSers automatically places order with supplier
   - Check order status in DSers dashboard

### Spocket Auto-Fulfillment:

1. **Enable Auto-Fulfillment**
   - In Spocket dashboard, go to **Settings**
   - Enable **Auto-Fulfillment**
   - Set payment method

2. **Configure Settings**
   - Set inventory sync frequency
   - Configure shipping preferences
   - Set up email notifications

---

## Step 6: Set Up Shipping

1. **Go to Shipping Settings**
   - In Shopify admin: **Settings** ‚Üí **Shipping and delivery**

2. **Create Shipping Zones**
   - Click **Manage rates**
   - Add shipping zones (e.g., "United States", "Europe")
   - Set shipping rates:
     - **Free shipping** (if offering)
     - **Flat rate** (e.g., $5.99)
     - **Weight-based** (if applicable)

3. **Configure Delivery Methods**
   - **Standard shipping**: 7-15 days (AliExpress)
   - **Express shipping**: 2-5 days (US/EU suppliers)
   - **Free shipping**: Over $150 (already set in your theme)

4. **Set Up Shipping Profiles** (if needed)
   - Different rates for different products
   - Go to **Shipping** ‚Üí **Shipping profiles**

---

## Step 7: Configure Payment Processing

1. **Set Up Shopify Payments** (Recommended)
   - Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Payments**
   - Click **Complete account setup** for Shopify Payments
   - Enter business details
   - Verify identity

2. **Alternative Payment Methods**
   - **PayPal**: Enable in Payments settings
   - **Apple Pay / Google Pay**: Enable if available
   - **Buy now, pay later**: Klarna, Afterpay, etc.

3. **Test Payment**
   - Use Shopify's test mode
   - Or place a small test order
   - Verify payment processing works

---

## Step 8: Set Up Inventory Management

1. **Enable Inventory Tracking**
   - Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Products**
   - Enable **Track quantity**
   - Set low stock alerts

2. **Sync Inventory** (DSers/Spocket)
   - In app settings, enable **Inventory Sync**
   - Set sync frequency (daily recommended)
   - App will update stock levels automatically

3. **Set Low Stock Alerts**
   - In Shopify: **Settings** ‚Üí **Notifications**
   - Enable low stock email alerts
   - Set threshold (e.g., alert when < 10 units)

## Step 9: Configure Product Information

### For Your Theme's Sustainability Features:

1. **Add Product Tags**
   - Go to each product
   - Add tags like:
     - `sustainable`
     - `organic-cotton`
     - `eco-friendly`
     - `recycled`

2. **Add Metafields** (Optional - for sustainability metrics)
   - Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Custom data** ‚Üí **Products**
   - Create metafields:
     - `carbon_saved` (number)
     - `water_saved` (number)
     - `materials` (text)
   - Add values to products

3. **Add Product Descriptions**
   - Write detailed, SEO-friendly descriptions
   - Include sustainability information
   - Add size guides, care instructions

## Troubleshooting

### Products Not Importing:
- Check app permissions in Shopify
- Verify supplier connection
- Check product availability

### Orders Not Auto-Fulfilling:
- Verify auto-fulfillment is enabled
- Check payment method in app
- Review order logs in app dashboard

### Inventory Not Syncing:
- Enable inventory sync in app settings
- Check sync frequency
- Manually sync if needed

### Shipping Issues:
- Verify shipping zones configured
- Check product weight/dimensions
- Review shipping rates
